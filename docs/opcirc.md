The gilt will not pay a coupon on 7 June 2000 but will pay a first coupon on 7 December 2000, which will be slightly larger than the nominal annual coupon to account for the additional interest from 25 May 2000 to 7 June 2000. The value of the coupon payable on 7 December (a "long" coupon) will be \pounds 2.275956 per £100 nominal
